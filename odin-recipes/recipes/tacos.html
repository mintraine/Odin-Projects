Tacos

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or flour tortilla folded or rolled around a filling.

Fillings include seasoned meats, beans, vegetables, and cheese, often topped with salsa, cilantro, or lime.

Perfect for casual meals, family dinners, or parties!

Ingredients

  • Corn or flour tortillas (soft or hard-shelled)
  • Protein: seasoned beef, chicken, pork, fish, or beans/vegetables
  • Taco seasoning (store-bought or homemade)
  • Toppings:
    • Lettuce, tomatoes, onions, jalape√±os, cilantro
    • Cheese: cheddar, Monterey Jack, queso fresco
    • Sauces: sour cream, guacamole, salsa varieties

Steps

  1. Cook taco meat until fully done, drain fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water, simmer until flavorful.
  3. Warm tortillas in oven or skillet.
  4. Assemble tacos with meat, toppings, and cheese.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
