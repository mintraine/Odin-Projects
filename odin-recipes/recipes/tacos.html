Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients, including meat, cheese, vegetables, and sauces.

They are versatile, easy to customize, and perfect for quick meals, parties, or casual family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Protein: Ground beef, chicken, pork, or beans for a vegetarian option.
  • Tortillas: Soft flour or corn tortillas.
  • Toppings:
    • Lettuce, diced tomatoes, onions
    • Shredded cheese
    • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
    • Salsa or hot sauce
    • Avocado or guacamole
  • Seasonings: Chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

Steps

  1. Cook the protein in a skillet over medium heat, adding seasonings to taste.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a pan or oven until soft and pliable.
  3. Assemble the tacos: add the cooked protein to the tortilla, then layer with desired toppings.
  4. Serve immediately with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on the side.
