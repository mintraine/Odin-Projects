Sushi

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish combining seasoned rice with fresh ingredients like seafood, vegetables, or egg.

It can be served in bite-sized rolls (maki), slices over rice (nigiri), or hand-rolled cones (temaki).

Freshness, simplicity, and artistic presentation make sushi both beautiful and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Short-grain Japanese sushi rice
  • Nori sheets (seaweed)
  • Rice vinegar, sugar, salt
  • Fillings:
    • Sushi-grade salmon or tuna (or cooked crab sticks, shrimp, smoked salmon)
    • Vegetables: avocado, cucumber, carrots

Steps

  1. Cook sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Mix rice with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  3. Place nori on bamboo mat.
  4. Spread rice evenly on nori.
  5. Add your fillings.
  6. Roll sushi tightly using bamboo mat.
  7. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
