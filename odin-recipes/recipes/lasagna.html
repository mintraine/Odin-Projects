Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of wide pasta sheets layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and savory meat or vegetables.

Baked until golden and bubbly, it has a comforting balance of flavors and textures.

Each slice offers a warm, filling meal that’s perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Noodles: Lasagna noodles (use no-boil or regular, cooked al dente)
  • Meat Sauce:
    • Ground meat (ground beef and/or Italian sausage)
    • Aromatics (onion, garlic)
    • Tomatoes (crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, or marinara sauce)
    • Seasonings (salt, pepper, dried basil, oregano, parsley, sugar)
  • Cheese Fillings:
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Eggs
    • Dried Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan, drain the fat, and stir in the tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a little Parmesan cheese in a bowl to make the cheese filling.
  4. Layer in a baking dish: spread some meat sauce on the bottom, add noodles, then ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers until the dish is full, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 30–40 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
