Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made of wide pasta sheets layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and savory meat or vegetables.

Baked until golden and bubbly, it has a comforting balance of flavors and textures.

Each slice offers a warm, filling meal that’s perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (no-boil or cooked al dente)
  • Ground meat (beef, Italian sausage, or combo)
  • Onion, garlic
  • Tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, dried basil, oregano, parsley, sugar
  • Ricotta cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Dried parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain.
  2. Brown the ground meat in a pan, drain fat, stir in tomato sauce, season with salt & pepper.
  3. Mix ricotta, egg, and a little Parmesan for cheese filling.
  4. Layer in a baking dish: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, repeat until full, finish with sauce & cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
